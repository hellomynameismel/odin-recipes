A bottle of Cynar goes a long way

This is such a delightful (read: easy and delicious!) drink that you can have anytime.

Is it warm out? Put it over ice (cubes or even shaved like a snow cone), and top with sparkling water and an orange slice. Refreshing!

Is it cold right now? Put it over one large ice cube, top with sparking water, and spritz the oil from an orange peel before dropping it in. It is sure to warm your insides.

Ingredients

  • 2-3 oz Cynar
  • sparkling water (or seltzer)
  • orange
  • ice

Steps

  1. Place ice in a glass.
  2. Pour in the cynar.
  3. Top with sparkling water. Adjust ratio to your liking.
  4. If it tastes too bitter, squeeze some juice from the orange and stir.
  5. Garnish with an orange slice or a spiral of orange rind.

Enjoy!
